Katsu Sando

Ingredients

  • 6 crispy burger patties
  • 3 slices tasty bread cut in half
  • 1 cup cabbage shredded
  • 1/4 cup katsu sauce
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a pan. Fry both sides of the crispy burger patties using medium heat until crispy. Set aside
  2. Grab 2 slices of bread. Spread butter on one side of the first slice of bread and mayonnaise on one side of the second bread slice.
  3. Lay down the bread slice with butter on a flat surface (butter side up). Top with shredded cabbage and tonkatsu sauce.
  4. Arrange 2 crispy Burgers over it and then top with more shredded cabbage and tonkatsu sauce. Finish by topping with the other bread slice (mayo side facing down).
  5. Put-in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Arrange Katsu Burger Sando on a serving plate. Serve. Share and enjoy!
