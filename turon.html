Turon

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces bananas saba or plantains, cut in half (lengthwise)
  • 1 cup jackfruit ripe, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 12 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 2 cups cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Fold and lock the spring roll wrapper, use water to seal the edge
  2. In a pan, heat the oil and put-in some sugar. Wait until the brown sugar floats
  3. Put-in the wrapped banana and fry until the wrapper turns golden brown and the extra sugar sticks on wrapper
  4. Serve hot as a dessert or snack. Share and Enjoy!
